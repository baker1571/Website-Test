Writer, Educator, German Specialist

Hello! I am Bill. I am interested in translating my experience in higher education into an industry position in Instructional Design, Communications, or Digital Marketing. Although I enjoy working in education, I am also a flexible self-starter, who is interested in acquiring new skills. My stellar student evaluations and peer reviews attest to the success of my teaching career. Students have consistently described my classes as “well organized,” “knowledgeable,” and possessing a “professional, yet approachable” learning atmosphere. I intend to bring this same degree of diligence and professionalism to my new position.

Since 2013, I have been teaching German language and culture at the college level. Last year, I was a Full-Time Instructor at the University of Kentucky. During my graduate studies at Ohio State, I taught introductory and graduate German language as well as co-designed and co-taught cultural courses addressing film and literature.

I dedicate a lot of my time these days to reading in a broad range of topics, everything from history and philosophy to HTML and Python programming. I am always interested in learning new information and acquiring new skills. In order to organize and digest this new information, I maintain a Zettelkasten for the sake of personal knowledge management.

I enjoy learning new languages. In undergrad, I majored in German and Russian with a minor in Linguistics. From there, I studied Japanese during grad school and began studying Mandarin in 2018. In addition to maintaining my language skills, I am currently working to acquire speaking and reading proficiency in French. Not only does this hobby demonstrate my perseverance and problem-solving ability, but I would love to put these skills to work in a professional setting.

I am excited about the prospect of bringing my talents to a new position. Please find my résumé, portfolio, and LinkedIn (as well as my dissertation if you should feel so inclined) at the links below.
